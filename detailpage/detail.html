Beach Hotel

Overlooking its private beach along the Red Sea, this 5-star hotel offers spacious suites with a private terrace.

Each comes with free Wi-Fi, a glass-walled bathroom with a marble bath and a separate shower. Some include a pool in the garden and iPod docking stations as well. Massages and various beauty treatments, including exfoliating scrubs, are offered. Other activities encompass star gazing, cooking class, yoga and aromatherapy and the 24-hour reception staff can arrange snorkelling trips and car rentals for guests.

Middle Eastern, Oriental, Indian, and international cuisine are offered at the restaurant which offers panoramic views of the beach and the Red Sea

Facilities

ROOMS.

All rooms sleep 2 adults and have a lounge area, balcony or terrace, king size bed, walk-in shower, WC, hair dryer, air conditioning, safe, telephone*, satellite TV and mini-bar*.
